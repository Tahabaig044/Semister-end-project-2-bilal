achievements
1. Nobel Prize in Physics (1979)
Awarded: Dr. Abdus Salam, Sheldon Glashow, and Steven Weinberg were jointly awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1979 for their contribution to the unification of the weak force and electromagnetic interaction between elementary particles. This achievement formed part of the Standard Model of particle physics, which is central to modern physics.
