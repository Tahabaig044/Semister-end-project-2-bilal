1. Electroweak Theory

Published: 1979

Description: This groundbreaking work by Dr. Abdus Salam co-authored with Steven Weinberg and Sheldon Glashow introduced the electroweak theory. This theory unified electromagnetism and the weak nuclear force, a critical advancement in the Standard Model of particle physics.
